Union of the Non-Magi

The union of the non-magi is the union in the kingdom that works and serves the people without magic. They are a group that wants to give equal opportunity to everyone who can't use magic. Their main function is to serve as giving the magicless a bigger say in what happens in the city. So that their voices matter. Only about 15% of the population doesn't have the ability to use magic so, their purpose is to help make life fairer for the those without. They actually have a spot on the council where they hold a lot of reputation.
