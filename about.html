What is Wandermere?

Wandermere is a sword and sorcery fantasy city that is going to become the setting for a bigger project that I am working on. I plan on making a game senior honors project that will feature the city of Wandermere as its setting.

Where is Wandermere located?

Wandermere is located in the world of Iglucia, which is a world that I have started making as a creative project. I have been working on this world for about 2 months. Originally, it started as a campaign setting for Dungeons and Dragons, but I decided to scrap that project and I have decided that this is how I will be using the world.

Why is it important?

Wandermere is important to me because it is a fairly large creative endeavor that I am pursuing. When you make something, it is very dear and near to you, this is the same for me and this project. Now, to make it important for you as an audience to find it interesting. I have to find a way to make it easy for you all to invest yourselves in this world. I want to make the world life like, and I want it to seem like these people are real. They need to be believable; they can be outrageous but in a believable way.
