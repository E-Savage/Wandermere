Balthazar Walstio

Birth Date - November 14, 098
Occupation - King
Associated Places - Palace
Spouse - Helia Walstio
Children - N/A

Information

He was born to the King and Queen of the land of Iglucia. Born as the 3rd child to the previous king having to fight for his current position in a magic tournament. Being the strongest mage in the family it was the best choice for him to become king. He ascended the throne, but his siblings still want to try to take it from him. His older sister and brother still look for their own opportunities to take the throne from him.

Now, he must not only defend his throne from his enemies but from his own siblings as well. Balthazar wants to change his kingdom for the better, but he must look over his shoulder all of the time.
