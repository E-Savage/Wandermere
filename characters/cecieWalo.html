Associated Places - Palace

Biography

Cecie was born to a tribe in the woods whose magic is rooted deep into the world. The magic of the Walo clan is highly infused with natural magic from the earth itself. Over tens of generations, they have been able to use the magic. The clan has decided to dedicate the magic they have to helping the world and to protect it. Cecie and the rest of her family are known for their duties of protecting the world. Cecie advocates for the world and her clan when she attends meetings and other official events.
