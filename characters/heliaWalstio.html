Helia Walstio

Birth Date - December 16, 109
Occupation - Queen
Associated Places - Palace
Spouse - Balthazar Walstio
Children - N/A

Biography

Born to a peasant family Helia was not inline to become Queen but she is a wise and powerful mage. Due to her own prowess, she was able to rise up through the ranks of her mage school. She excels so far that she finds herself on the city council by the age of 15, which is extremely young to become a council member, but this is where she meets a young Prince Balthazar. This is where their epic story begins.

They began to practice their magic with each other, and this is where Balthazar learns to use his best magic. Through all of this their bond grows stronger and stronger. Until the point that they decide to tie the knot and next thing you know, she has become the queen.
