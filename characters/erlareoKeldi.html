Biography

The origins of Keldi are rather mysterious, he has been around for centuries. He is one of the oldest creatures in existence. He has the appearance of being human, but he has other non-human tendencies. He hardly sleeps, requires very little food and water and only eats raw meat, seems almost reptilian in a way. So far, he has posed no threat to the kingdom, but who knows what he sees as the future of the kingdom.
