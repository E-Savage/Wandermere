Biography

Malia was born to a clan that was native to the forest regions of the country. The Ocwulf family actually has a specific trait that gives them the slight appearance of a wolf. Her family uses a special form of bestial magic that has been passed through generations.

Her family is now known as those who protect the forest, they have made it their duty to do so. The spiritual guardians of the forest also help them with their quest.
