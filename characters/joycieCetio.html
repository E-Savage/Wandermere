Biography

Joycie was orphaned at a young age but adopted by a wealthy family she was able to practice and develop her magic in an environment that excelled her forward in her studies and practices. The family that took her made sure that she was able to have an upper hand due to her raw potential.

Being given that extra help she was able to excel herself forward and move on with all of her studying and practices. She is now known as one of the strongest mages in all of the realm.
