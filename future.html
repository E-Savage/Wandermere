My thoughts about the Project

This setting will be used in a game at some point that I am making for a senior honors project. I might also use this setting for a Dungeons and Dragons campaign. There are stories and games that this setting can be used to make.

I think this setting will be very rich and full of material that can be used for people to use in their own creative projects. I will allow the use of material here for the other people to use but whatever is posted to this website by me is what is considered to be canon.

My project is here to help those who may be a little lost in their own project and do not know where to go next. But that is the purpose of this website, while I work on my own projects from this setting, I am creating others can use this material to make their own worlds.

I want to help people in their own creative endeavors, and I feel that this is the best way to do it. My fellow writers, I wish you all the best with your own creative writings!
