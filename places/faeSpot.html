Fae Spot Tavern

Information about Place

The Fae Spot Tavern is one of the most well-known spots in town. This is where everyone goes in the evenings to cool down from the day, even the baker comes here to cool down after baking all day long. The tavern hosts fun activities to do such as darts, axe throwing, and on occasion karaoke. There is also live music usually and patrons are allowed to bring their own games, cards are a very common game that is brought here.

There is a food special every day, and the kids also eat for free every Tuesday night!
