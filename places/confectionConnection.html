Confection Connection Bakery

Information about Place

The confection connection is a family-owned bakery in the middle of Wandermere. This is also the same Bakery that caters events at the palace. It is one that is loved my many, this is place everyone wants to go to have their morning coffee and morning pastries. Confection Connection is a wonderful place that allows us to satisfy our sweet tooth.

There is a special coffee special at 2pm every afternoon, make sure you bring a friend!
